About Me

I am a musician and composer with a passion for narrative and atmospheric compositions. I create sonic landscapes that act as settings for stories.

Katabasis

My most recent project – Katabasis – is a collection of compositions inspired by the story of Aeneas, an ancient Roman mythologigal hero who, during his travels, delved into the underworld in pursuit of knowledge.\n\n Each composition represent a location or a character encountered by Aeneas in the underworld, and captures, through my compositional lense, the emotion that Aeneas experienced during each of these interactions.
