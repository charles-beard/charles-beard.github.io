Katabasis

My most recent project – Katabasis – is a collection of compositions inspired by the story of Aeneas, an ancient Roman mythologigal hero who, during his travels, delved into the underworld in pursuit of knowledge.

Each composition represents a location or a character encountered by Aeneas in the underworld, and captures, through my compositional lense, the emotion that Aeneas experienced during each of these interactions.

I tracked and edited each of these pieces within 24 hours in order to force myself to adhere to a single musical idea from start to end; each piece acts as a moment within the greater narrative, rather than as a narrative itself.

This collection is also a study in sonic timbre and repetitive layering. I wanted to allow the addition of new timbres to recontextualize existing layers, creating a soundscape that is dynamic, but which can fade into the atmosphere through repetition.
